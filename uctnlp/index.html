UCT NLP

University of Cape Town Natural Language Processing Group

We perform research in natural language processing and machine learning. Research topics include: methods for automatic text generation, language modelling and machine translation; supervised and unsupervised models for discovering linguistic structure in text; and developing datasets and NLP models for low-resource languages, with a particular focus on African and South African languages.
