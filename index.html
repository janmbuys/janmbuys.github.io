About

I am a Lecturer (â‰ˆ Assistant Professor) in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Cape Town. My research area is Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Washington, working with Yejin Choi. I completed my PhD at the University of Oxford, supervised by Phil Blunsom. Before that I obtained a masters degree in Computer Science and undergraduate degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Stellenbosch in South Africa.

My current research focusses on linguistic structure prediction, text generation, and low-resource language processing. I am particularly interested in approaches that incorporate more structure into neural network-based models.

Reviewing (2020): ACL, EMNLP, NeurIPS, SACAIR, TACL.

Teaching (2020): CSC1019 Intro Programming; CSC4025Z Artificial Intelligence.
